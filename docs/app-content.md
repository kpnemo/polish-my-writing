## Long description (for the website / Release body)

**Polish My Writing** lives quietly in your menu bar and fixes your writing
wherever you type — Mail, Slack, Notes, your browser, anywhere you can select
text. Highlight a sentence, press a hotkey, and it's instantly replaced with a
cleaner version: grammar, spelling, punctuation, and awkward phrasing fixed —
while keeping your voice, your meaning, and your original language. It's a polish,
not a rewrite.

There's no account and no subscription. You bring your own API key (Anthropic,
OpenAI, or OpenRouter), and your text goes straight from your Mac to the provider
you chose — never through anyone else's server. It's free, and it's yours.

## Key features

- **Works in every app** — anywhere you can select text.
- **One keystroke** — select, press ⌥⌘P, done. No copy-paste, no switching windows.
- **Keeps your voice & language** — polishes, never rewrites; works in any language.
- **Bring your own key** — Anthropic, OpenAI, OpenRouter, or Google Gemini. Your account, your control.
- **Private by design** — no backend, no telemetry; your API key stays in the Keychain.
- **Out of the way** — menu-bar only, with a guided first-run setup.
- **Free.**

## How it works (3 steps)

1. **Select** text in any app.
2. **Press ⌥⌘P** (or your own shortcut).
3. The selection is **replaced** with a polished version.

## Requirements

- macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later.
- An API key from Anthropic, OpenAI, OpenRouter, or Google Gemini (free tiers available).
- Accessibility permission (macOS asks on first launch — it's what lets the app
  replace your selected text).

## Privacy (one-liner + link)

Your text and your API key go directly from your Mac to your chosen LLM provider
over HTTPS — there is no server in between, and nothing is stored or tracked. See
the full [Privacy Policy](../PRIVACY.md).
